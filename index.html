About

One bookkeeper. Your full attention.

I'm an ICB (Institute of Certified Bookkeepers) Level 3 qualified bookkeeper, working directly with small business owners, sole traders and contractors across Ireland. No account managers, no hand-offs — when you call, you speak to the person who actually does your books.

My practice is built on three things: accuracy, clear deadlines, and plain-English explanations. I work with the major Irish accounting platforms and keep you compliant with Revenue requirements all year round, not just at tax time.
